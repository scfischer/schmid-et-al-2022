\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Creates a visual representation of oxygen molecules diffusing from alveolar space into the capillary. 

This class is used to visualizes the flow of oxygen into the capillary, using the number of crossing particles from the {\ttfamily crossing\+Spheres\+O2} array provided by Partial\+Pressure. They are distributed randomly across the the length of their given capillary section. The particles are created in the alveolar space and move towards the center of the capillary, using target points on the {\ttfamily Path\+Creator} path through the capillary. To represent absorption into the blood, the particles fade to transparent as they move into the capillary.
